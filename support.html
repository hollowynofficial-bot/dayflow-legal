Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open Nevixo and sign in using your email address. Your data will then be securely synchronized across your devices.

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account and its associated data from the Settings section inside the app. You may also use our Account Deletion page if available.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled both in Nevixo and in your device settings. If reminders are disabled, the app cannot send notifications.

How do I sync my data?

Simply sign in with the same account on each device. Your information will automatically synchronize through your account.

Can I use Nevixo offline?

Yes. Most productivity features remain available offline. Some cloud features require an internet connection.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please send us an email describing the issue. Including your device model and operating system version helps us investigate more quickly.

How can I suggest a new feature?

We love hearing your ideas. Send your suggestions to our support email and we'll consider them for future updates.
