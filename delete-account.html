Delete Your Nevixo Account

Last updated: July 2026

Nevixo allows you to permanently delete your account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Nevixo app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

If you are unable to access your account, you may also request account deletion by contacting us at:

hollowynofficial@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your account information.
  • Your tasks, habits, projects, notes, and other synchronized data.
  • Your application settings associated with your account.

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be temporarily retained when required by law or for security purposes. Any retained information is kept only for as long as necessary.

Contact
