Nevixo Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 2026

Nevixo respects your privacy and gives you full control over your personal data. You may permanently delete your account and all associated personal information at any time.

Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the Nevixo app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

If you cannot access your account, you may request deletion by contacting us:

Email: hollowynofficial@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following information is permanently removed:

  • Your account information.
  • Your email address.
  • Your tasks.
  • Your habits.
  • Your projects.
  • Your notes.
  • Your synchronized application data.
  • Your application preferences and settings associated with your account.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain technical information may be retained temporarily if required:

  • to comply with applicable laws;
  • to prevent fraud or abuse;
  • for security or backup purposes.

Any retained information is kept only for the minimum period required and is not used for advertising purposes.

Deletion Time

Your account deletion request is processed as soon as reasonably possible. Any remaining backup copies are automatically removed according to our retention policies.
